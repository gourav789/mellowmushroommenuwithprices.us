Privacy Policy

How themellowmushroommenu.us collects, uses, and protects information.

Last updated: June 2025

1. About This Website

This privacy policy applies to themellowmushroommenu.us, an independently operated informational website. We are not affiliated with Mellow Mushroom, Home Grown Industries of Georgia, Inc., or any of their franchise operators.

2. Information We Collect

Information You Provide

When you use our contact form or newsletter signup, we collect the information you provide, including your name and email address. This information is used only to respond to your inquiry or send you the newsletter you requested.

Automatically Collected Information

Like most websites, we may collect certain technical information automatically when you visit, including: your IP address, browser type and version, operating system, referring website, pages you view, and time spent on those pages. This information is used in aggregate form to understand how visitors use our site and to improve its content and usability.

3. Cookies

This website may use cookies — small text files stored on your device — to improve the browsing experience. These may include performance cookies that help us understand how the site is used and preference cookies that remember your choices. You can control cookie settings through your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect some site functionality.

4. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to: respond to contact form submissions, send newsletters to subscribers who opt in, improve our website content and user experience, and analyze site traffic in aggregate. We do not sell personal information to third parties. We do not use personal data for advertising purposes.

5. Third-Party Links

Our site contains links to external websites including mellowmushroom.com, DoorDash, Uber Eats, Grubhub, and other third-party services. These websites have their own privacy policies. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of any external websites linked from this site.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable steps to protect information submitted through this website. However, no internet transmission or electronic storage method is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security of information transmitted to or through this website.

7. Children's Privacy

This website is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us and we will remove it promptly.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information we hold about you. To exercise these rights, contact us through the contact form on this website. We will respond to requests within a reasonable timeframe.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of this website after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

Questions about this privacy policy? Contact us through our contact page.
